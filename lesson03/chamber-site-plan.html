Nelson Mandela Municipality Chamber of Commerce

This name is given to my local chamber of commerce because of Nelson Mandela who is the country freedom fighter hero. And his name is associated with peace, values,and it is attarctive for tourism and investments as the city is his birthplace as well.

Site Purpose

Nelson Mandela Chamber of Commerce fosters business unity among its members represents and defend its business interests.It promotes the Locale attractions in order to bring in more tourists in the region and facilitates the establishement of new bisinesses and investments.It encourages new membership and give regular bulletin updates about the economy and business opportunities in the region.
